Sushi

Description of the sushi:

This sushi was finely made, the ingredients from the pure coasts of Japan.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Fish
  • Vegetables
  • Seaweed

How to make it!

  1. Put rice onto a sushi roller
  2. Layer it with seaweed
  3. Put your fillings (fish and vegetables) onto half of the seaweed
  4. Roll it up
  5. Chop it into your preferred width
  6. Enjoy!
