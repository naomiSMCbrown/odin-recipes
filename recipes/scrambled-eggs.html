Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Description of Scrambled Eggs:

This delicate dish is the perfect breakfast appetizer, it goes with nearly anything.

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • A splash of milk
  • Salt
  • Oil or butter

How to make it:

  1. In a bowl, crack and whisk the eggs
  2. Add a splash of milk and salt into it
  3. Transfer the egg into a hot pan on the stove after putting oil or butter on it
  4. wait for edges to cook
  5. Once the edges are solid and yellow, begin folding and playing with the egg to create a scrambled look
  6. Once cooked, remove from pan and serve :0
